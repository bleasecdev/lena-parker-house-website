Our Guests

Stay

Guests will enjoy a range of beautifully appointed rooms and suites to meet all of their needs. Each room has been meticulously designed and furnished to ensure the utmost comfort and style.

Relax

Guests will experience a spa featuring tranquil treatment rooms, each appointed with soft lighting, soothing music, and comfortable furnishings. Our expert therapists will provide a wide range of massage therapies, body treatments, facials, and other spa services, all designed to help guests feel relaxed and refreshed.

Eat

The atmosphere in the restaurant will be warm and welcoming, focused on creating a memorable dining experience for our guests. Whether they're celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying a night out, our restaurant will be the perfect destination for an unforgettable dining experience.

Play

The hotel will feature a high-end bar with a bowling alley, providing guests with a unique and unforgettable experience. The bar features a sleek and modern design, with a wide selection of craft beers, wines, cocktails, and other premium beverages.

Explore

Guests will take advantage of a high-end car service that provides a premium transportation experience. Our car service is designed to cater to the diverse needs of our guests, whether they are traveling for business or leisure.

Connect

Our members and guests come from a range of backgrounds and industries, including tech, finance, entertainment, and more. They are entrepreneurs, CEOs, artists, and influencers who are shaping the future of their industries and making a positive impact on the world. There will always be someone interesting to meet at Lena Parker House.

Our Mission

Our mission at Lena Parker House is to provide an unparalleled experience of luxury, comfort, and indulgence. As a boutique hotel & spa, restaurant, and social club, we are committed to offering world-class amenities and exceptional service to our guests. Our goal is to create a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere that caters to the needs and desires of our guests, while also providing an escape from the ordinary. We are dedicated to upholding the highest standards of excellence in all aspects of our operations, from our facilities to our staff, and to making a positive impact in our community. At Lena Parker House, we strive to exceed our guests' expectations and provide them with an unforgettable journey of refined elegance and elevated living that will stay with them long after their visit to our establishment.

Our Founder

Cares Perry

Visionary

Cares Perry, is a highly accomplished individual with an exceptional background in the creative arts. After obtaining a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Studio Arts from Virginia State University in 2013, she went on to establish herself as a sought-after talent in the field of set design, event curation, and creative direction at top advertising agencies in the world. Driven by her passion for creating unique and unforgettable experiences, Cares embarked on the ambitious project of building a luxury boutique hotel & spa, restaurant, and social club that combines her love for design, hospitality, and luxury living. Her commitment to excellence and attention to detail are reflected in every aspect of Lena Parker House, which promises to deliver an unparalleled experience of refined elegance and indulgence to its guests.
